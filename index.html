FEATURES

Amazing Features

Realistic Disaster Simulations

Experience natural and man-made disasters powered by advanced physics. Practice response strategies and understand how disasters behave in real-world conditions.

Education-Focused Scenario Packs

Prebuilt scenarios created with domain experts and verified data. Each scenario includes objectives, context, and learning outcomes.

Scenario Editor

Create custom disaster scenarios using an intuitive editor. Adjust variables like location, disaster type, intensity, timing, and available resources.

Physics-Driven Environment

Every structure, object, and weather system follows realistic physical rules. See the real impact of decisions and test different response approaches safely.

Performance & Decision Analysis

Review timelines, decisions, and outcomes after each simulation. Learn from mistakes, improve strategies, and build better decision-making skills.

Multiplayer Collaboration

Join shared simulations with up to 64 participants. Coordinate roles, communicate in real time, and practice teamwork. Train as a team, simulate multi-agency responses, and improve coordination skills.

About DisasterX

A cutting-edge disaster simulation platform that bridges the gap between education and entertainment.

Our Mission

To create the most realistic and educational disaster simulation experience that empowers communities, first responders, and organizations to prepare for real-world emergencies through immersive gameplay and cutting-edge technology.

Our Vision

A world where disaster preparedness is accessible to everyone through innovative simulation technology. We envision DisasterX becoming the global standard for disaster response training and coordination.

Why DisasterX?

An immersive platform that teaches disaster response, teamwork, and systems thinking while delivering high-fidelity gameplay and creative scenarios — now with anime-inspired visuals and neon UI skins.
